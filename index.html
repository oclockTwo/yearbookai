Introduction to yearbookai

Yearbook AI is a trend that emerged on social media where individuals post photos that resemble 1990s high school yearbook pictures. This trend is driven by an app named EPIK - AI Photo Editor, which facilitates the creation of these retro-style photos using artificial intelligence. Here’s a detailed rundown of the Yearbook AI trend and the EPIK app:

Yearbook AI Trend:

The trend involves individuals posting photos on social media that look as if they were taken for a 1990s high school yearbook. These photos are not real yearbook pictures; instead, they are generated using artificial intelligence. The generated photos feature '90s hairstyles, clothing, and the generic blue-grey backdrop characteristic of yearbook photos from that era.

EPIK - AI Photo Editor App:

  • EPIK is the primary app fueling this trend. It's a smartphone application that combines generative AI with traditional editing tools, allowing users to manipulate photos or their own likeness in various creative ways. A notable feature in EPIK is the AI Yearbook tool, which automatically generates the '90s-style yearbook photos.
  • To utilize the Yearbook feature, users need to upload between 8 to 12 selfies and select their gender. Upon payment of an in-app purchase fee, the app generates the yearbook images. The EPIK app is free to download, but generating AI yearbook photos requires a payment, with packages costing up to $9.99.
  • The EPIK app gained substantial popularity, hitting No. 1 on the App Store, with users and influencers globally sharing their AI-generated yearbook photos on social media.

Reception and Popularity:

  • The AI Yearbook photo trend took social media by storm around October 2023, with many users and even some celebrities sharing their AI-generated '90s yearbook photos online.
  • EPIK, which stands for “Excellent Pictures Ink”, was launched in 2022 and rose to fame due to its AI Yearbook feature that convincingly generates retro-styled portrait photos.

This trend showcases a creative and nostalgic use of AI, allowing individuals to reminisce and engage with a stylized past while sharing these generated memories on social platforms.
